Top 10 Healthy Snacks for Busy People

Busy schedule? No worries! Here are 10 healthy snacks you can grab on the go without compromising nutrition:

  1. Apple slices with peanut butter 🍎
  2. Greek yogurt with honey 🍯
  3. Trail mix with nuts & dried fruits 🌰
  4. Whole grain crackers with hummus 🥙
  5. Protein smoothie 🍓
  6. Veggie sticks with guacamole 🥑
  7. Boiled eggs 🥚
  8. Roasted chickpeas 🌿
  9. Popcorn (air-popped, not fried) 🍿
  10. Dark chocolate with almonds 🍫

✅ These snacks are quick, tasty, and fuel your body with energy while keeping hunger away.
