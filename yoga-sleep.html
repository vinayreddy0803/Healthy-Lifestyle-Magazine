5 Yoga Poses for Better Sleep

Struggling with sleep? These yoga poses relax the body and calm the mind, preparing you for restful sleep:

  1. 🧘‍♀️ Child’s Pose (Balasana) – Relieves stress & fatigue
  2. 🪷 Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani) – Improves circulation
  3. 🌙 Reclining Butterfly Pose – Calms the nervous system
  4. 😌 Supine Spinal Twist – Relieves tension in the back
  5. 🛌 Corpse Pose (Savasana) – Deep relaxation before sleep

✨ Try these poses for 10–15 minutes before bed and notice the difference in your sleep quality.
