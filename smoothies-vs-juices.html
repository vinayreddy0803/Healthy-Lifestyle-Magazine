Smoothies vs Juices: Which is Healthier?

Both smoothies and juices are healthy choices, but they serve different purposes. Let’s compare:

🥤 Smoothies

  • ✅ Keeps fiber intact → helps digestion
  • ✅ More filling (great for breakfast)
  • ⚠️ Can be high in calories if too much fruit/sugar added

🍹 Juices

  • ✅ Easy to absorb nutrients quickly
  • ✅ Great for hydration
  • ⚠️ Removes fiber, leading to faster sugar spikes

👉 Verdict: **Smoothies are better for meals**, while **juices are good for quick refreshment**. Balance is key!
