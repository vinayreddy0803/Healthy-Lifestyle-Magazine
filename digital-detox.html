Digital Detox: Why You Need It

Our phones and screens are part of daily life, but too much screen time harms both mental and physical health. A digital detox can help restore balance.

💡 Benefits of Digital Detox

  • ✨ Better sleep and reduced eye strain
  • ✨ Improved focus and productivity
  • ✨ Stronger real-life connections
  • ✨ Lower stress and anxiety

📌 Tips for Digital Detox

  1. Set “no phone” times (like before bed)
  2. Try a social media break for a week
  3. Spend more time in nature 🌿
  4. Replace scrolling with reading or hobbies 📖

🌸 Even small steps toward less screen time can make your life calmer and healthier.
